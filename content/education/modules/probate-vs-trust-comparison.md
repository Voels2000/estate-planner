## Educational Comparison Framework

### Probate-Oriented Path

- Court-supervised process
- Timeline and process vary by state and complexity
- Public-record implications may apply

### Trust-Oriented Path

- Private administration framework (subject to governing law)
- Requires proper trust funding to be effective
- Trustee administration discipline remains essential

## Key Coordination Point

Most households need both document quality and implementation quality. Even strong documents can fail if ownership, beneficiary designations, and administration are not aligned.

## Discussion Prompts for Professionals

- Which current assets are likely probate-exposed?
- Which trust-funding gaps exist today?
- What administrative responsibilities should successors prepare for?

> Educational note: transfer mechanics are jurisdiction-specific and should be reviewed by licensed legal counsel.
