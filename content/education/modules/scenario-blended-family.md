## Scenario Context

A blended family household is balancing spouse security, children from prior relationships, and fairness across branches.

## Typical Friction Points

- Beneficiary misalignment across accounts and documents
- Differing expectations between spouse and children
- Executor/trustee selection challenges

## Common Educational Takeaways

- Coordination between trust terms and beneficiary forms is critical
- Communication and role clarity reduce conflict risk
- Scenario modeling helps identify where assumptions diverge

> Educational note: use this scenario as a discussion framework with your advisor and estate attorney.
