## Annual Exclusion Gifting Basics

Annual exclusion gifting can be used to transfer value over time without using lifetime exemption in many standard cases.

## Practical Planning Questions

- Who are the intended recipients?
- What assets are being transferred?
- Is there a recurring gifting cadence?
- How should records be maintained for audit/compliance support?

## Coordination Notes

Annual gifting often interacts with broader estate, tax, and cash-flow strategy.

Document assumptions and implementation steps before execution.

> Educational note: tax treatment depends on current law and personal facts; consult qualified professionals.
