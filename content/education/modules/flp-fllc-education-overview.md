## What FLP/FLLC Concepts Cover

Family limited partnerships and family LLC structures are often discussed in education contexts for governance and transfer planning.

## Common Educational Themes

- Ownership units and control rights
- Governance and operating discipline
- Transfer restrictions and valuation discussion points

## Planning Questions

- What governance model is sustainable for the family?
- How should documentation and record-keeping be handled?
- How does entity planning coordinate with tax and estate assumptions?

> Educational note: entity implementation and valuation conclusions require licensed legal and tax guidance.
