## Why Charitable Trusts Come Up in Planning

Charitable trust structures are often discussed when families want to align philanthropic goals with transfer and tax planning.

## Common Educational Framework

- **CRT concepts**: charitable remainder orientation
- **CLAT concepts**: charitable lead orientation
- Integration with broader estate and income-tax assumptions

## Key Planning Topics

- Cash-flow implications over trust term
- Beneficiary and charitable payout expectations
- Administrative complexity and trustee responsibilities

## Coordination Considerations

Charitable structures should be evaluated in a cross-disciplinary conversation with legal, tax, and financial professionals.

> Educational note: this module is informational and not a recommendation for any specific structure.
