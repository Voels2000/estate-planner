## What Is Estate Planning?

Estate planning arranges how assets are managed if you become incapacitated and how they transfer at death.

## Five Core Documents

1. Will
2. Revocable trust
3. Durable power of attorney
4. Healthcare directive
5. HIPAA authorization

## Two Common Gaps

- Beneficiary designations that are outdated or inconsistent
- Asset titles that do not match intended transfer outcomes

## Why Coordination Matters

Estate planning, tax planning, and retirement planning are connected. Inconsistent decisions across these areas can create avoidable costs and delays.
