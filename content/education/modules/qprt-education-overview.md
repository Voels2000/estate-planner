## What a QPRT Is (Education Context)

A Qualified Personal Residence Trust is commonly discussed as a residence-transfer planning structure with defined term mechanics.

## Typical Discussion Topics

- Transfer timing and retained-use period
- Property valuation assumptions
- Ongoing occupancy and administrative considerations

## Practical Trade-Off Areas

- Flexibility vs planning certainty
- Property-use expectations over long horizons
- Coordination with broader liquidity and transfer plans

> Educational note: QPRT structuring requires legal design and individualized tax review.
