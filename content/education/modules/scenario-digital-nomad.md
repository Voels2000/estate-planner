## Scenario Context

A mobile professional works remotely across multiple states while accumulating assets in different jurisdictions.

## Common Planning Tensions

- Unclear domicile evidence over time
- State-level tax and residency ambiguity
- Document and beneficiary coordination across jurisdictions

## Educational Takeaways

- Domicile documentation discipline matters
- Residency assumptions should be reviewed periodically
- Multi-state facts should be organized before advisor meetings
