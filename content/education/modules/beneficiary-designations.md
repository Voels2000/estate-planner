## Why Beneficiary Designations Matter

Beneficiary designations control transfer for many assets, including retirement accounts and life insurance.

In many cases, the beneficiary form controls the transfer **even if a will says something different**.

## Accounts Commonly Controlled by Beneficiary Form

- IRA and 401(k) accounts
- Life insurance policies
- Annuities
- Transfer-on-death and payable-on-death accounts

## Common Mistakes

- Ex-spouse still listed
- No contingent beneficiary listed
- Designations not coordinated with trust strategy
- Inconsistent percentages across accounts

## Good Review Practice

- Review designations after major life events
- Coordinate designations with your estate attorney and financial advisor
- Keep primary and contingent beneficiaries current
