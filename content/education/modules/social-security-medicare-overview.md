## Social Security (Educational Overview)

Claiming age decisions can influence lifetime benefits and survivor outcomes.

Timing analysis should be coordinated with other income sources, tax projections, and household longevity assumptions.

## Medicare (Educational Overview)

Medicare planning involves enrollment timing, coverage choices, and ongoing cost considerations.

Common planning focus areas:

- Initial enrollment windows
- Parts A/B/C/D distinctions
- Out-of-pocket and supplemental coverage trade-offs

## Coordination Topics

- Interaction with retirement withdrawals
- Income-sensitive premium impacts
- Long-term care planning context

> Educational note: claiming and enrollment decisions are individualized and should be reviewed with licensed professionals.
