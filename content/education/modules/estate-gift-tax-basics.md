## Federal Transfer Tax Framework

Federal estate and gift tax planning uses a unified lifetime exemption framework under current law.

The practical tax impact depends on total estate composition, prior taxable gifts, and applicable rules at transfer dates.

## Gift Tax Basics

- Annual exclusion gifting may reduce taxable estate over time
- Larger lifetime gifts can consume exemption amounts
- Certain direct tuition and medical payments may receive special treatment under current rules

## Step-Up in Basis Concept

Under current federal law, many inherited assets receive basis adjustment to date-of-death value, which can materially affect future capital gains tax outcomes.

## State-Level Differences

Some states impose estate tax or inheritance tax at thresholds much lower than federal levels.

Always evaluate state domicile and situs interactions in parallel with federal strategy.

> Educational note: tax law changes and personal facts can materially alter outcomes; coordinate with qualified tax and legal professionals.
