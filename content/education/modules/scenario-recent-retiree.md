## Scenario Context

A recently retired household needs to coordinate withdrawals, taxes, healthcare costs, and legacy preferences.

## Core Questions

- Which accounts should fund spending first?
- How should tax brackets and RMD timing be managed?
- How do retirement decisions affect estate transfer goals?

## Educational Takeaways

- Retirement income and estate outcomes are tightly linked
- Annual review cadence helps reduce drift from assumptions
- Documented decision rationale improves advisor continuity

> Educational note: this scenario is educational and should be paired with personalized professional analysis.
