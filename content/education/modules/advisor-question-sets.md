## Estate Attorney Questions

- Which documents are essential for my current family and asset structure?
- Which assets are likely to pass outside my will today?
- How should trust terms and beneficiary designations be coordinated?

## Financial Advisor Questions

- What are my top planning priorities over the next 12-24 months?
- How do my tax, retirement, and estate decisions interact?
- Which assumptions should we stress-test first?

## CPA / Tax Advisor Questions

- What are the biggest tax inefficiencies in my current plan?
- How should we evaluate gifting vs retention for appreciated assets?
- Which state and federal assumptions need annual review?

## How to Use This Module

- Pick your top 5 questions before each meeting
- Bring your current assumptions and recent life changes
- Document action items and owner after each meeting

> Educational note: these prompts support informed discussions and do not replace professional advice.
