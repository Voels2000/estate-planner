## Financial Readiness

- Current income and expense summary
- Debt snapshot and interest rates
- Insurance policy list and key coverage details
- Top near-term goals (12-24 months)

## Retirement Readiness

- Account balances by type
- Target retirement timeline
- Social Security questions and assumptions
- Withdrawal and tax concerns

## Estate Readiness

- Status of core legal documents
- Beneficiary and titling review notes
- Priority transfer goals and concerns
- Known family or executor coordination issues

## Meeting Output Checklist

- Decisions made
- Open questions
- Owner for each action item
- Review date for follow-up

> Educational note: checklists support preparation and do not replace professional advice.
