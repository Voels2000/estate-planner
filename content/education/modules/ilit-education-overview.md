## What an ILIT Is

An Irrevocable Life Insurance Trust (ILIT) is commonly used in education discussions as a structure for life insurance ownership and transfer planning.

## Typical Objectives

- Potential estate inclusion separation objectives
- Liquidity support planning for heirs or estate obligations
- Structured trust-based benefit distribution

## Planning Considerations

- Premium funding workflow and administration
- Trustee duties and communication expectations
- Coordination with broader estate document set

## Common Risk Areas

- Incomplete administration practices
- Mismatch between policy assumptions and long-term goals
- Poor coordination with beneficiary and liquidity strategy

> Educational note: ILIT implementation requires legal counsel and careful execution discipline.
