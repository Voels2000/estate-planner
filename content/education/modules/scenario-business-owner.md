## Scenario Context

An owner has substantial wealth concentration in a private business and wants continuity plus equitable family outcomes.

## Typical Decision Areas

- Succession timeline and leadership transition
- Valuation assumptions and transfer pricing sensitivity
- Liquidity planning for taxes or equalization goals

## Educational Takeaways

- Succession planning and estate planning cannot be separated
- Governance and documentation quality materially affect outcomes
- Cross-functional advisor coordination is essential

> Educational note: this scenario is illustrative and not implementation advice.
