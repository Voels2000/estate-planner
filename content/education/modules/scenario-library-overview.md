## Why Scenario Learning Helps

Scenario-based learning makes abstract planning concepts easier to apply.

The goal is not to copy another household's strategy, but to identify patterns that may be relevant for your own professional discussions.

## Example Scenario Types

- Blended family with inheritance complexity
- Business owner with valuation and succession questions
- Retired couple balancing income, healthcare, and transfer goals
- Multi-state household with domicile and tax coordination issues

## How to Use Scenarios

1. Identify which scenario is most similar to your current stage
2. Note the top 3 decisions that drove outcomes
3. Bring those decision points to your advisor/attorney/CPA meeting

> Educational note: scenarios are composite illustrations and not individualized recommendations.
