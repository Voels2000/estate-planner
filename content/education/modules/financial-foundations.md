## What Is Financial Planning?

Financial planning is an ongoing process of setting goals, understanding your current position, and making decisions that support your life over time.

It is not just one investment account or one product. It is a connected system.

## The Six Pillars

1. Cash flow and budgeting
2. Risk management and insurance
3. Investment strategy
4. Tax planning
5. Retirement planning
6. Estate planning

## Why This Matters

A decision in one pillar often changes the outcomes in other pillars. For example, retirement withdrawals can affect taxes, and tax choices can affect estate transfer outcomes.
