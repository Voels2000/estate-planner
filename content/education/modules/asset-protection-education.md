## What Asset Protection Means

Asset protection education focuses on lawful risk-reduction structures and governance practices, not concealment or improper transfer.

## Common Educational Themes

- Liability segregation concepts
- Insurance and umbrella coverage coordination
- Entity governance and documentation discipline
- Timing and intent considerations

## Important Boundaries

Asset protection planning must be prospective, lawful, and coordinated with qualified legal counsel.

## Practical Preparation

- Maintain clean records and ownership documentation
- Clarify personal vs business liability exposures
- Review coverage adequacy alongside legal structure

> Educational note: implementation strategy is legal advice territory and requires licensed counsel.
