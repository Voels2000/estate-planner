## Retirement Planning Basics

Retirement planning focuses on how to replace employment income over a potentially long time horizon.

## Core Income Sources

- Social Security
- Employer plans (401(k), 403(b), pensions)
- Personal savings and brokerage assets
- Other sources (business income, real estate, annuities)

## Key Risks to Plan For

- Longevity risk (outliving assets)
- Inflation risk (loss of purchasing power)
- Sequence-of-returns risk (early negative returns)
- Healthcare and long-term care costs

> Educational note: claiming, withdrawal, and tax sequencing decisions are personalized and should be reviewed with qualified professionals.
