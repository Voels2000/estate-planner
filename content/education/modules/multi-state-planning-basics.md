## Why Multi-State Planning Is Complex

Different states can apply different rules for estate tax, inheritance tax, and residency-based obligations.

## Key Educational Concepts

- Domicile vs residence distinction
- State situs considerations for certain assets
- Planning implications when relocating later in life

## Typical Review Inputs

- Current and planned domicile timeline
- Asset location and ownership structure
- State-specific transfer tax thresholds and rules

> Educational note: multi-state planning is fact-specific and requires licensed legal and tax review.
