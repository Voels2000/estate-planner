## Why Succession Planning Matters

Business owners face both continuity and transfer-planning questions that affect family outcomes, tax planning, and operating stability.

## Core Succession Topics

- Ownership transfer timing and structure
- Governance and decision authority continuity
- Valuation assumptions and documentation discipline
- Funding and liquidity for transition events

## Coordination Checklist

- Align legal documents and entity agreements
- Clarify successor roles and responsibilities
- Coordinate tax assumptions with CPA and legal strategy

> Educational note: succession planning involves legal, tax, and operational design and should be handled by qualified professionals.
