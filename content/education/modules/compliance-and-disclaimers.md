## Educational-Only Boundary

This platform is designed for educational support and planning literacy.

It is not legal, tax, investment, or financial advice.

## When to Escalate to Professionals

- Significant tax exposure or uncertain assumptions
- Trust drafting or legal document execution decisions
- Social Security/Medicare timing with material income impact
- Business succession or multi-state domicile complexity

## Why This Matters

Good educational tooling helps you ask better questions and prepare better inputs.

Licensed professionals are still required for personalized recommendations and implementation.
