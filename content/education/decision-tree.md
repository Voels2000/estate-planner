# Self-Guided Decision Tree

Use this path to identify which educational topics to prioritize first.

## Step 1: Family Structure

- Single
- Married/partnered
- Blended family
- Dependents or special-needs support

## Step 2: Tax Exposure

- Primarily income-tax planning needs
- Potential estate-tax exposure
- Multi-state domicile/tax questions

## Step 3: Asset Profile

- Primarily cash and market investments
- Business ownership
- Significant real estate
- Concentrated stock or illiquid assets

## Step 4: Planning Goal

- Protect family and simplify transfers
- Reduce tax drag
- Retirement income confidence
- Legacy and charitable impact

## Suggested Learning Paths

- **Foundations first:** Financial Foundations -> Retirement Foundations -> Estate Foundations
- **Estate-forward:** Estate Foundations -> Beneficiary & Titling topics -> Tax deep-dive
- **Retirement-forward:** Retirement Foundations -> RMD/Tax sequencing -> Estate coordination

> Educational note: this path is a learning framework, not personalized advice.
