# Glossary

## Beneficiary Designation

A legal naming of who receives an account or policy at death.

## Durable Power of Attorney (DPOA)

A document authorizing someone to act on financial matters for you if you cannot.

## HIPAA Authorization

A release allowing designated people to receive your medical information.

## Probate

Court-supervised process to settle an estate and transfer certain assets after death.

## Revocable Trust

A trust that can usually be changed during your lifetime and can help avoid probate when properly funded.

## RMD (Required Minimum Distribution)

Minimum annual amount that must be withdrawn from certain retirement accounts after a specified age.

## Step-Up in Basis

Tax basis adjustment of inherited assets to fair market value at death under current law.

## Titling

The legal form of ownership for an asset (individual, joint, trust, entity, etc.).
